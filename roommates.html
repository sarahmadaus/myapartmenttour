hi, I'm Sarah!

I'm a graduate student at Pratt Institute in New York City, seeking a Masters degree in Library and Information Sciences. Aside from my studies, I'm a freelance writer, brand consultant, and copywrite. You can find my work in Sports Illustrated, Architectural Digest, Town & Country, Allure, Oprah Daily, Well+Good, SELF, The Quality Edit, and more. My brand clients include Peloton, Atlas Coffee Club, Swim USA, and Elix Healing.

I'm also an indoor cycling instructor at Equinox. Previously, I spent more than three years at Conde Nast's SELF magazine, first as the editorial assistant, and then as a commerce writer covering new product launches, curating gift guides, and writing service content with e-commerce at the forefront. I'm a 2019 Magna Cum Laude graduate of Temple University in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. I received my B.A. in Journalism with a minor in Business Studies.

During my last year at Temple, I founded the university's first women's lifestyle magazine, REFINE. With a full masthead of dedicated editors, marketing assistants, writers, and designers, I led the team to create two issues during the 2018-2019 academic year and set them up for success once I graduated. Throughout my undergraduate career, I was also the Director of Communications and Publicist, respectively, for two Temple Student Government administrations, as well as the Photography Editor of the Templar Yearbook.

On the personal side of things, I live in Bed-Stuy (obviously), and spend a lot of time reading, taking care of my plants, frolicking with my partner's dog Ruby, and working on jigsaw puzzles while I binge watch early-2000s teen dramas.

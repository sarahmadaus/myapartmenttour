About Our Home

Hi there! We're Sarah and Kara, two journalists, fitness instructors, and armchair interior designers. We moved into our beloved Bed-Stuy brownstone in August 2022 after getting drunk on a Hudson River booze cruise and deciding to become roommates. Over the past year, we've done dozens of projects to make this home truly ours. This website exists to show you around our space, get to know us, and provide you with some inspiration for your own rental home.
